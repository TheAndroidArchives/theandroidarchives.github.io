Description

Jimmy Five and Smudge are always trying to take Monica's plush rabbit Samson! This time they actually did it! But Monica found out soon enough! She just won't let them go without learning the lesson!

Play as Monica as you go after the boys in this Great Adventure! They will hide in crazy places but you have some tricky manouvers to hit them with Samson!

- Adjust strenght and angle to throw Monica's plush rabbit Samson!

- Lots of different maneuvers to hit the boys.

- Win the challenge of each level earning all stars.

- New levels will always be arriving.

- Bonus level will test your quickness!

New Monica's Gang games will arrive soon! Be sure to check often!
